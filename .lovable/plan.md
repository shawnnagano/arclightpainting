

## SEO/AEO H1 Optimization for Color Consultation Page

### Problem
The current H1 — "ColorConfidence Consultation" — is a branded term with zero search volume. Nobody searches for "ColorConfidence." People search for **"paint color consultation"**, **"paint color consultant near me"**, and **"color consultation for painting."**

### Recommended H1

```
Paint Color <span class="text-accent">Consultation</span>
```

**Why this wins for SEO + AEO:**
- Targets the exact search query "paint color consultation" (high local intent)
- Follows the site pattern of keyword-first H1s (like "House Painting Cost")
- Short, scannable, and unambiguous for AI answer engines
- Per your SEO rules, no "WA" in H1 — Bothell is covered in the meta title and H2

### What moves where
- **"ColorConfidence™"** drops into the **tagline** line: `The ColorConfidence™ Experience` or stays in the existing tagline as-is
- The **meta title** already has "ColorConfidence™" + "Bothell" — no change needed there

### Changes
1. **`src/pages/ColorConsultation.tsx`** — Update the H1 from `Color<span>Confidence</span> Consultation` to `Paint Color <span>Consultation</span>`

One line change, significant SEO impact.
